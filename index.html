The Blind Side

"The Blind Side" is a story about a homeless black teen, Michael Other, who has drifted in and out of the school system for years. Then Leigh Anne Tuohy and her husband, Sean, takes him in. The Tuohys eventually becomes Michael's legal guardians, transforming both his life, as well as theirs. Michael's tremendous size and protective instincts works together, on the highschool football team, transforming him into a force to be reckon with on the field! With the help he receives from his new family and devoted tutor, he realizes his potential as a student and football player.
